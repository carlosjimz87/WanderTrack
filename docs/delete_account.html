Self-service Account Deletion

You can delete your WanderTrack account directly from the app at any time. This action is permanent: all your visited-places data and profile info will be erased.

  1. Open the app and go to your Profile screen.
  2. Scroll down and tap the red Delete Account button.
  3. In the confirmation dialog, tap Delete to remove your account.

By Email Request

Or send an email to carlosjimz87@gmail.com with subject "Delete my WanderTrack account", including:

  • Your registered email address.
  • Statement confirming you want all your data removed.

We will reply within 30 days to confirm deletion.
